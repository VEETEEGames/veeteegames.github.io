Project Jazzgame

Privacy Policy

Effective Date: August 18, 2025

1. Introduction

Welcome to Project Jazzgame. Your privacy is very important to us. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect information when you use our game.

By playing Project Jazzgame, you agree to the practices described in this policy.

2. Information We Collect

When you use the game, we may collect:

  • Personal Information: We do not collect personally identifiable information such as your name, email, or phone number.
  • Non-Personal Information: We may collect data such as device type, operating system, gameplay statistics, and crash reports to improve the game experience.

Additionally, our game integrates with third-party services:

  • Google Play Services: Our game uses Google Play Services for achievements, leaderboards, and cloud saving. Google may collect and process data as described in their Privacy Policy.

    Google Play Services Privacy Policy
  • AdMob (Advertising): We use AdMob to display ads in the game. AdMob may use cookies, device identifiers, and usage data to provide personalized or non-personalized ads.

    AdMob Privacy Policy

3. How We Use Information

We use collected data to:

  • Improve gameplay experience and fix issues.
  • Provide achievements, leaderboards, and cloud saves through Google Play Services.
  • Show ads to support the free availability of the game.

4. Children’s Privacy

Our game is not directed at children under 13. If you are under 13, please do not use the game without parental consent. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

If you believe we have collected information from a child, please contact us and we will take steps to remove it.

5. Data Sharing and Security

  • We do not sell or trade your personal data.
  • Information collected by AdMob and Google Play Services is managed by Google according to their own policies.
  • We take reasonable measures to keep your data safe.

6. Your Choices

  • You can manage ad personalization through your Google account:

    https://www.google.com/settings/ads
  • You can sign out of Google Play Services at any time if you do not wish to use achievements, leaderboards, or cloud saves.

7. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Updates will be posted within the game or on the Google Play Store page. Please review it periodically.
