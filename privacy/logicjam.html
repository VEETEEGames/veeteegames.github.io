Logic Jam

Privacy Policy

Effective Date: January 5, 2025

1. Introduction

Thank you for using Logic Jam. Your privacy is important to us. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you play our game.

By using Logic Jam, you agree to the terms described in this Privacy Policy.

2. Information We Collect

We do not directly collect or store personal information from users. However, Logic Jam uses third-party services such as Google AdMob which may collect data to serve advertisements.

Data collected by Google AdMob may include:

  • Device Information (IP address, device type, operating system)
  • Advertising Data (used for personalized or non-personalized ads)

For more information, please refer to Google’s Privacy Policy:

Google Privacy Policy

3. How We Use Information

  • To serve personalized and non-personalized ads
  • To improve gameplay experience and performance
  • To analyze app usage and stability

4. Third-Party Services

Logic Jam uses Google AdMob for advertising purposes. These third-party services may use cookies and similar technologies.

For more details, review:

Google AdMob Policies

5. Children’s Privacy

Logic Jam does not knowingly collect personal information from children under the age of 13.

If you believe such data has been collected, please contact us immediately so we can delete it.

6. Data Security

We take reasonable steps to protect user information. However, no system is 100% secure.

We rely on trusted third-party services such as Google to manage data securely.

7. Your Choices

  • You can opt out of personalized ads through Google Ads Settings
  • You can disable cookies in your device or browser settings

Manage Google Ads Settings

8. Changes to This Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy may be updated from time to time. Changes will be posted within the game or on this page.

Please review this page periodically for updates.
