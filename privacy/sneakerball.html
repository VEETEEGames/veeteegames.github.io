Privacy Policy

Effective Date: January 5, 2025

1. Introduction

Thank you for using Sneaker Ball. Your privacy is important to us. This Privacy Policy explains how information may be collected, used, and protected while using the game.

By using Sneaker Ball, you agree to the practices described in this Privacy Policy.

2. Information We Collect

VEETEE Games does not directly collect or store personal information from users.

However, Sneaker Ball uses third-party advertising services such as Google AdMob, which may collect certain information to serve advertisements.

Information that may be collected by Google AdMob includes:

  • Device Information: Such as IP address, device type, and operating system.
  • Advertising Data: Information used to provide personalized and non-personalized advertisements.

For more details regarding how Google collects and processes data, please review their Privacy Policy:

Google Privacy Policy

3. How We Use Information

Information collected through Google AdMob may be used to:

  • Serve personalized and non-personalized advertisements.
  • Improve user experience through relevant content.
  • Analyze app performance and usage trends.

4. Third-Party Services

Sneaker Ball uses Google AdMob to manage in-game advertisements.

These third-party services may use cookies and similar technologies to collect information and improve advertising services.

Additional information regarding Google AdMob policies can be found here:

Google AdMob Policies

5. Children’s Privacy

Sneaker Ball does not knowingly collect personal information from children under the age of 13.

If you believe that personal information from a child has been inadvertently collected, please contact us immediately and we will take appropriate action to remove it.

6. Data Security

We are committed to protecting user information.

While VEETEE Games does not directly collect or store user data, we rely on trusted third-party services such as Google to handle data responsibly and securely.

7. Your Choices

  • Opting Out of Personalized Ads: You can manage your advertising preferences through your Google Ads settings.
  • Disable Cookies: Cookies can be disabled through your browser or device settings to limit certain types of data collection.

Manage your ad settings here:

Google Ads Settings

8. Changes to This Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy may be updated from time to time.

Any changes will become effective immediately after the revised policy is posted within the game or on this page.

Users are encouraged to review this Privacy Policy periodically for updates.
